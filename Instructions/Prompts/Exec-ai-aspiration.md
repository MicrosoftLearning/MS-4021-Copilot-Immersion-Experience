## Define Your AI Priorities

Define a clear **12-month AI aspiration** and identify the **highest-impact initiative** to accelerate value.

In this exercise, Copilot acts as a **strategic thinking partner** to help you clarify outcomes, identify opportunities, and focus your efforts.

You’ll complete three steps:

- Define your AI aspiration (12 months)
- Identify key AI initiatives
- Prioritize one initiative to take forward

> **NOTE:** Sample prompts are provided to help you get started—feel free to personalize them for your role, organization, and priorities.

## Step 1: Define Your AI Aspiration (12-month)

Start by identifying a **high-impact business outcome** that AI could drive over the next year.

Focus on outcomes that would represent a meaningful shift in how your organization operates or delivers value.

### 1. Identify Your Priority Outcome

- Open a new browser tab and navigate to [m365.cloud.microsoft/chat](https://m365.cloud.microsoft/chat).

- Ensure the **Work mode** tab is selected in **Copilot Chat**.  

   ![Screenshot showing Work mode tab in Copilot Chat.](../Prompts/Media/work-mode.png)  

    **Sample Prompt:**
  
    ``` text
    For my [insert business unit, function, or organization], identify the top 2–3 business challenges or opportunities where AI could materially drive value over the next 12 months.
    
    Focus on organization-level outcomes — not individual productivity.
    
    For each opportunity, capture:
    -Title
    -Business problem or opportunity
    -Why it matters
    
    Then define:
    -1–2 measurable success metrics 
    -A clear AI aspiration
    ```

    > **Capture your response** in Word, OneNote, or Copilot Pages. You will use these notes in the next steps.

### 2. Define What Success Looks Like

Describe the measurable outcomes or KPIs that would indicate success if your AI aspiration were realized.

**Sample Prompt:**

``` text
If we successfully achieved this AI aspiration, what measurable outcomes or changes would indicate success? Provide examples of metrics or observable improvements.
```

> **Capture your response** for use in the next steps.

### 3. Select AI Value Areas

Review the four AI value areas and identify which would most directly advance your aspiration:

## Step 2: Identify Your AI Initiatives

Now break this aspiration into smaller, actionable initiatives.

### 1. Generate Initiative Ideas

**Sample Prompt:**

``` text
Based on this AI aspiration:

[paste your aspiration]

Suggest 2–3 practical AI initiatives that could help achieve this outcome.
```

### 2. Capture Your Initiatives

Write down:

- **One existing initiative contributing to this aspiration**
- **One new opportunity that could accelerate progress toward it**

> Keep this short — just a name and a one-sentence description for each.

## Step 3: Prioritize Your Focus

Select the initiative most likely to deliver measurable value in the next 90 days.

### Identify Your Gap and Focus

``` text
Given my AI aspiration and these initiatives:

[paste your aspiration and initiatives]

What is the biggest gap or risk that could prevent success?

Which initiative would create the most measurable value in the next 90 days, and why?
```

### Final Output

By the end of this exercise, you should have:

- One clear **AI aspiration (12-month outcome)**
- Two initiatives (**current + new**)
- One **prioritized initiative** to take forward

> You will use this prioritized initiative as your entry point to begin delivering on your broader AI aspiration through a focused 90-day plan.
